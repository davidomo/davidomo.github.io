JD Salinger wrote The Catcher in the Rye as almost a stream of thoughts of a troubled young man. I used to love that book. Now, I'm too afraid to read it again. i've changed, and that angsty, rebellious teen that I was may not be the same person who reads it. I'm scared, truely, that I have become too dark and cynical in my way-too-young-to-be-truly-middle-aged mindset.
